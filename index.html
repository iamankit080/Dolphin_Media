about us

Crafting Moments That Last Beyond the Event

Dolphin Media has been creating truly special celebrations since January 12, 2001. We are a trusted BTL agency known for blending creativity, quality, and value for money to deliver memorable brand experiences.

Our passionate team brings diverse expertise and years of experience to craft customized BTL strategies that keep your goals at the center. From concept to execution, we focus on campaigns that engage audiences and build meaningful connections.

With a proven track record of impactful brand activations and long-term partnerships, Dolphin Media goes beyond service — we collaborate to create experiences that connect, engage, and inspire.
